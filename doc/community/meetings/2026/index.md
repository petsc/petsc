# 2026 PETSc Annual Users Meeting

```{image} https://www.firedrakeproject.org/_static/banner_firedrake_petsc_26.png

:width: 800
```

The 2026 PETSc annual user meeting will be hosted by the Firedrake Team in **Oxford, UK**, during the week of **June 1~5, 2026**. PETSc occupies the first half of the meeting (June 1-3, 2026), for which you can register, but we encourage everyone to attend the whole week as many amazing things are going on in Firedrake Land.
The following link has all details including registration. Notice that students can register for the whole week, _including room and food_, for only &pound; 400.

Please see details here: <https://www.firedrakeproject.org/firedrake_26.html>
